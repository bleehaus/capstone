question

Are you a chatbot?

practice buddy

Yes. I'm essentially a chatbot. RiveScript by Noah Petherbridge is a script for writing chatbots. The best way to practice using someone's pronouns is with a real person, but some don't have that option. I'm programmed specifically to help you to practice using someone's pronouns and remind you when you slip up.

question

When should I practice with you?

practice buddy

Has your friend recently asked you to start using different pronouns for them? Has a peer recently asked to be called a different name? Are you rusty or confused? We've all been there, and we just need some repeated practice.

question

Why should I practice with you?

practice buddy

As Trevor J. Sangrey put it, learning someone's pronouns often requires mentally taking them out of one bucket and putting them into another. I'm here to help you make that change.

question

What are other things I can do to practice?

practice buddy

You can list someone's pronouns in your phone contact. You can practice using their pronouns in your head, like "I texted [name] today. [Pronoun] is so cool and loves cats." You can ask a friend to listen to you as you talk about this person (that's the idea behind this).
